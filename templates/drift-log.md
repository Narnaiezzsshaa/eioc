# EIOC Drift Log

**Longitudinal Record of Emotional-Layer Events**

---

## Purpose

The Drift Log captures emotional-layer events over time, enabling:
- Pattern recognition across incidents
- Identification of high-risk channels or contexts
- Detection of operators who may need additional support
- Evidence collection for compliance and audits

---

## Entry Template

Copy this template for each drift event:

```
### Entry #___

**Date / Time:**  
**Operator:**  
**Channel:**  

**Drift Type:**
- [ ] Fog — "I couldn't think clearly"
- [ ] Narrowing — "I could only focus on one thing"
- [ ] Confusion — "Nothing made sense"
- [ ] Emotional flooding — "I was overwhelmed"
- [ ] Disorientation — "I lost track of the conversation"

**Trigger:**  
_What initiated the drift? (e.g., rapid questions, authority claim, urgency)_

**Boundary Distortion Present:** ☐ Yes  ☐ No  
**Verification Performed:** ☐ Yes  ☐ No  

**Resolution:**  
_How was the situation resolved?_

**Follow-Up Required:** ☐ Yes  ☐ No  
_If yes, describe:_

---
```
